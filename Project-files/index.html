About me

My name is Alex, I am 31 years old and I currently reside in Athens, Greece. I have studied Sound Engineering and Music Production but I recently decided to switch fields, aspiring to build a career on Web Development and Programming.

Last year, I begun studying Web Development and Programming at the Public Vocational Training Institute of Zografou (Athens). It is a two-year long learning program, focused on the practical aspect of computer sciences, where I am being trained to apply modern technologies in a variety of relevant fields. What is more, I have completed several online seminars on major learning platforms like Coursera, Udemy etc and I intend to continue to do so, since there is always something new to learn. I also read and study as many things I can and I am constantly developing personal projects in order to get practical experience and improve my skills. I am currently focusing on front-end web development and technologies like HTML5, CSS3 (Bootstrap 5, Sass etc.), Javascript and on getting more and more familiar with tools like various IDEs, Github etc. I also intend to acquire designing skills by studying Photoshop sometime in the future. Lastly, I am fluent in English which helps me quite a lot during my studies.

I was always fascinated by computers and the fact that by using them, one can do amazing things, from creating beautiful imaginary worlds to performing complex surgeries. While studying Sound Engineering, I had the chance to see how digital sound tools and software are made and used and I found myself being more interested in that aspect than in actual recording and producing. This ultimately led me to decide that I want to switch fields. Web Development and Programming offer the chance be part of the creative process of pretty much everything in the modern world. They offer the chance to build a meaningful career in rapidly developing industry with ample job opportunities, where you can constantly learn and work on new and interesting things and become part of an open-minded and industrious community. I think that finding something that can inspire you that much, is not a common thing nowadays.

What I am looking for, is a place to start. I would like to work, improve my skills and learn many others in a professional environment, as a part of a team. I know that since I am a "late-bloomer", I have to cover more ground faster, but I believe that my commitment, my determination and my focus can make up for my late start. Given a chance and hopefully some guidance, I believe that I can be a promising addition to a company's roster as a payed intern or an entry level employee. I still have one year left to complete my formal studies so I do have some time restrictions until I graduate. If you have such a position in mind, please contact me.
